<FeatureGrid class="mt-12">
  <FeatureItem icon="realtime" title="Real-time editing">
    Collaborate simultaneously during a meeting or workshop, in person or remotely. Changes appear instantly for all participants.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="dashboard" title="Dashboard">
    See the latest news, your current tasks, and upcoming meetings at a glance.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="roles" title="Roles and accountabilities">
    Define the purpose, domain, and accountabilities of each role for crystal-clear governance.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="decisions" title="Decisions">
    Record governance decisions within circles to keep a clear and accessible trail for everyone.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="recurring" title="Recurring meetings and templates">
    Schedule regular meetings and create reusable templates to save time at every session.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="calendar" title="Calendar integration">
    Sync your meetings with Google Calendar, Outlook, or any iCal-compatible calendar.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="search" title="Global search">
    Instantly find a member, role, discussion, task, or decision with built-in search.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="import" title="Data import">
    Import your organizational structure from various tools, including Holaspirit, to get started quickly.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="export" title="Org chart export">
    Export your org chart as a PNG image for presentations and internal documents.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="download" title="Data export">
    Export your organization's data in XLSX or JSON format. Choose which entities to include: members, roles, decisions, tasks, meetings, and more.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="notifications" title="Notifications and digest">
    Receive configurable summary emails (daily or weekly) to stay informed without being overwhelmed.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="activity" title="Activity log">
    View the complete history of all changes within your organization.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="governance" title="Protected governance">
    Restrict structural changes to circle leaders to secure your governance.
  </FeatureItem>

  <FeatureItem icon="opensource" title="Open source">
