## Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.rolebase.io](https://app.rolebase.io) and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password.
3. Confirm your email address via the link sent to your inbox.

You are now ready to create your first organization.

## Create Your Organization

1. From the dashboard, click **Create Organization**.
2. Give your organization a name.
3. Optionally set a custom **slug** (the short identifier used in your URL, e.g. `app.rolebase.io/orgs/my-org`).

Your organization starts with a single top-level role that you can rename and expand.

## Build Your Structure

Your organizational structure is made up of **roles**. A role that contains other roles acts as a **circle** (a team or department). Start by creating a few key roles:

1. Click on your top-level role to open it.
2. Click **Add** and select **Role** to create a new role.
3. Give each role a name, purpose, and accountabilities.
4. Add sub-roles to create circles.

<Callout type="tip" title="AI Suggestions">
  Rolebase can generate role suggestions using AI. Based on the parent role's purpose, the AI proposes sub-roles with pre-filled purpose, domain, and accountabilities.
</Callout>

## Invite Your Team

1. Go to the **Members** section of your organization.
2. Click **Invite** and enter the email addresses of your team members.
3. Each person receives an invitation email with a link to join.
4. Once they accept, assign them to their roles.

<Callout type="info" title="Pending Invitations">
  Invitations are automatically resent with decreasing frequency until accepted. You can track the status from the members list.
</Callout>

## Start Collaborating

With your structure and team in place, you can:

- [Schedule meetings](/en/docs/meetings) with structured agendas for your roles.
- [Create tasks](/en/docs/tasks) to track work items.
- [Open threads](/en/docs/threads) for asynchronous discussions.
- [Record decisions](/en/docs/decisions) to keep a governance log.
