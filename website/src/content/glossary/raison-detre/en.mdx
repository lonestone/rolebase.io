"Purpose," in an organizational context, constitutes the vital foundation of a circle or a role within a Role Based Management structure or similar framework. It represents the driving force behind the actions and decisions of that circle or role, while expressing its deeper meaning and essential contribution to the organization as a whole.

The purpose serves to anchor the activities and objectives of a circle or role within a broader perspective and to give meaning to its existence. It answers the fundamental question: "Why does this circle or role exist, and what is its unique contribution to the organization's overall mission?"

Defining the purpose goes beyond purely functional or operational aspects, exploring the added value that the circle or role brings to the organization and its stakeholders. It can encompass elements such as vision, values, culture, desired impact on society, or other aspects that reflect the fundamental reason for its existence.

Clarifying the purpose is essential for aligning the members of a circle or role with their mission and objectives, thereby strengthening their engagement and understanding of the importance of their actions. It also helps create an organizational culture where every element of the structure feels invested with a meaningful mission within the whole.

In summary, purpose represents the vital foundation of a circle or role within an organization, motivating its actions and expressing its deeper meaning. It plays a crucial role in aligning, engaging, and helping members of the organization understand the unique contribution of each element to the organization's overall mission.
