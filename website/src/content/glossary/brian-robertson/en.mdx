His contribution to the development of Holacracy has been significant, introducing an innovative approach to governance and business management.

Robertson is first and foremost a practitioner. He designed Holacracy in response to the challenges he encountered as a company leader. His method was developed in the field, which gives him particular credibility in the area of organizational change. However, his ideas have not been without controversy, and many experts and companies hold differing views on the effectiveness of Holacracy in various contexts.

As an author and speaker, Robertson has played an important role in spreading Holacracy. He has contributed to educating the public about this approach, sharing his experiences and ideas through his talks and writings. While some of his presentations have generated significant interest, others have also been criticized for lacking nuance.

The Holacracy method, designed by Robertson, offers a structured framework for managing organizations, but it is not necessarily suited to every company. Opinions on its effectiveness are mixed, and many organizations have faced challenges during its implementation.

In summary, Brian Robertson is a key figure in the world of organizational change, particularly regarding Holacracy. His ideas have sparked debates and discussions, and his role in spreading this method is undeniable. However, it is important to note that Holacracy is not a universal solution and that its effectiveness depends on the specific context and organizational culture.
