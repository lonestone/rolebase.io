A "checklist" is an organized and structured tool in the form of a list, used as an operational guide to ensure that all necessary tasks, steps, or items are completed systematically and thoroughly. Checklists are widely used across various fields to ensure consistency, accuracy, and compliance in the execution of processes, procedures, or projects.

Checklists can vary in size and complexity, ranging from simple task lists to detailed documents covering multiple aspects of an operation. They are commonly used in healthcare, aviation, project management, maintenance, safety, quality assurance, and many other sectors. Checklists help track and document progress, reduce human errors, facilitate training and onboarding, and ensure compliance with standards and protocols.

In summary, a "checklist" is a structured list that serves as an operational guide to ensure that all necessary tasks or steps are completed systematically. It is an essential tool for ensuring quality, compliance, and efficiency in processes and operations across many professional fields.
