## Overview

Rolebase integrates with external tools to keep your workflow connected. Currently supported integrations include calendar sync with **Microsoft Office 365** and **Google Calendar**.

## Calendar Integrations

Calendar integrations allow Rolebase meetings to appear directly in your preferred calendar app. When a meeting is created, updated, or deleted in Rolebase, the change is reflected in your connected calendar.

### Office 365

To connect Office 365:

1. Go to your account settings or the organization's apps section.
2. Select **Office 365**.
3. You will be redirected to Microsoft's login page.
4. Sign in and grant Rolebase permission to access your calendar.
5. Once authorized, Rolebase meetings are synced to your Outlook calendar.

### Google Calendar

To connect Google Calendar:

1. Go to your account settings or the organization's apps section.
2. Select **Google Calendar**.
3. You will be redirected to Google's consent screen.
4. Sign in and grant Rolebase permission to manage calendar events.
5. Once authorized, Rolebase meetings appear in your Google Calendar.

<Callout type="info" title="Permissions">
  Both integrations request only the minimum calendar permissions needed. You can revoke access at any time from your Microsoft or Google account settings.
</Callout>

## What Gets Synced

| Item | Synced? |
|---|---|
| Meeting title | Yes |
| Date and time | Yes |
| Recurring schedule | Yes |
| Participants | Yes |
| Meeting link | Yes |
| Tasks and threads | No (use Rolebase directly) |

## iCal Export

Even without connecting a calendar app, you can export meetings as **iCal feeds**. Copy the iCal URL and subscribe to it from any calendar app that supports `.ics` subscriptions. The feed updates automatically when meetings change.

<Callout type="tip" title="Subscribe, Don't Download">
  Use your calendar app's "subscribe to calendar" feature (not "import") so that updates are synced automatically.
</Callout>
