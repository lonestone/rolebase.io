## Overview

Rolebase offers tiered subscription plans to fit organizations of different sizes. Subscriptions are managed per organization and billed through **Stripe**.

## Plans

| Plan | Pricing | Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic features. Limited number of members. Ideal for small teams getting started. |
| **Startup** | Per seat/month | All core features. Suitable for growing teams that need full access to meetings, tasks, threads, and integrations. |
| **Business** | Per seat/month | Everything in Startup, plus advanced features, priority support, and higher limits. |

<Callout type="info" title="Per Seat Billing">
  Paid plans are billed per active member (seat) per month. Archived members do not count toward your seat total.
</Callout>

## What is Included in Each Plan

### Free Plan
- Organization creation
- Roles
- Basic meetings
- Tasks and threads
- Limited member count

### Startup Plan
- Everything in Free
- Unlimited members
- Meeting templates and recurring meetings
- Calendar integrations (Office 365, Google Calendar)
- iCal export

### Business Plan
- Everything in Startup
- Advanced governance features
- Priority support
- Higher storage and API limits

<Callout type="tip" title="Try Before You Subscribe">
  The Free plan is fully functional for small teams. You can explore all core features before deciding to upgrade.
</Callout>

## Managing Your Subscription

Only the organization **Owner** can manage the subscription.

### Subscribing

1. Go to your organization's settings.
2. Navigate to the **Subscription** or **Billing** section.
3. Select a plan.
4. Enter payment details via the Stripe checkout flow.
5. Your subscription is activated immediately.

### Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

- **Upgrading** takes effect immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.
- **Downgrading** takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

### Cancelling

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to billing settings.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, then reverts to the Free plan.

No data is lost when downgrading or cancelling. Your organization, roles, and history remain intact.

## Billing and Invoices

Rolebase uses **Stripe** for payment processing. From the billing section you can:

- View your current plan and next billing date.
- Update your payment method.
- Download **invoices** for past payments.
- View billing history.

<Callout type="info" title="Secure Payments">
  All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Rolebase never stores your credit card details.
</Callout>
